MidSummer Movie Madness Series

MidSummer's Most Spectacular Event

Emerald Park cordially invites you to the premier summer event, MidSummer Madness Movie Series!

Midsummer Madness Movie Series will begin August 6, 2016, in the meadow at Emerald Park, located in the heart of Midtown. Our theme for this season's event is Classic Movies. Come spend your nights watching classic movies with Hollywood's iconic movie stars and your friends - and make some new friends!

This summer's movies are listed below. We have a great selction of movies for everyone. Do not miss this summers movie madness.

August 6, 2016 ~ The Seven Year Itch

Midsummer Madness Movie Series begins with a comedy about Manhattan businessman Richard who sends his wife and son to Maine to escape the summer heat, The Seven Year Itch is perhaps most famous for the iconic scene with Marilyn Monroe in her white dress. Hilarity ensues as Richard tries to resist the temptation of the voluptuous Hollywood beauty.

August 13, 2016 ~ The Endless Summer

Our second movie is a classic documentary about surfers in search for the perfect wave, The Endless Summer transports viewers around the world to some of the most beautiful oceans and sunny, sandy beaches.

August 20, 2016 ~ One Crazy Summer

Our third movie, One Crazy Summer, is about an aspiring teenage cartoonist and his friends come to the aid of a singer trying to save her family property from developers

August 27, 2016 ~ Auntie Mame

The piece de resistance for this season is the 1958 box office smash hit, Auntie Mame. Mame Denis, a progressive and independent woman of the 1920s, is left to care for her nephew Patrick after his wealthy father dies. Mr. Babcock, Patrick's assigned executor, objects to Mame's unconventional way of living and tries to force her to send Patrick to prep school. As Mame and Patrick grow closer, Mr. Babcock tries to discipline Patrick and threatens to separate the two if Mame does not comply with his wishes. This movie is full of nincompoopery that will have you in stitches.

Rules & Etiquette

We would like to remind every one of our rules and some etiquette to remember while in Emerald Park.

  1. Blankets and beach chairs are welcomed for your viewing pleasure. To ensure viewing pleasure for all, lawn chairs are permitted in the back four rows.
  2. Age of admittance is 21 years or older. ID's will be checked upon entering.
  3. There is no smoking or "vaping" in the park.
  4. No open flame candles, tiki type torches or grills will be allowed. Citronella torches will be lit around the perimeter to repel mosquitos.
  5. Please be mindful and respectful of those around you
  6. Failure to follow rules and proper etiquette will result in your removal from the event.

We ask that you have a enjoyable and wonderful time during our event. If you need assistance, please reach out to those in the blue and green volunteer shirts.
